Stampbook

Each stamp on this page signifies an high achievement, regular accomplishment, or otherwise fun thing I've done. They can span especially serious things (getting an award, publishing a paper, submitting a patent, etc.) to everyday things (watching a show, visiting a new location, eating a new food, etc.). Feel free to suggest new stamps and to create your own using this link.
